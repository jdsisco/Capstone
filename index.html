Jamila Sisco - NEIT Capstone Project

Overview

Capstone Project: Winter 2019.

Costume Con 37 is a convention being held in Danvers, Mass from March 22nd to March 25th, 2019. This project is a database of the panels and workshops scheduled to occur, which attendees can view and sort into date, time, and room. They can also search for a particular subject or panelist.
